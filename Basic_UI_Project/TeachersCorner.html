Class Teachers

Mary Burke, Bachelor of Education.

Mary has been teaching in Bright Sparks Preschool since January 2000. She has been teaching in the newly established Autism Class since it opened in September 2011. Mary has completed a selection of CPD courses in the areas of Autism, the teaching of children with Special Needs and Behaviour Management with Hibernia College, ICEP and Special Educational Support services. In her free time Mary enjoys continuing her Professional Development in areas such as Drama, Music, Sport and Literacy and Numeracy. Mary is actively involved in community development.

Claire Burke, Bachelor of Education

Claire joined the Bright Sparks team in 2014, having previously taught in a variety of settings and classes for the past 3 years throughout Galway. She studied in Mary Immaculate College and has since completed different CPD courses, many in the area of Special Educational Needs, having a massive interest in this area. A huge sporting fan, Claire trains the local football team and is also a member of the team herself. Claire is very musical and plays a variety of instruments, including the piano, guitar and the fiddle. She has been involved with the Communion Choir, provided music for the Christmas concert and has helped to run various after school clubs such as tin whistle and basketball in our school.
